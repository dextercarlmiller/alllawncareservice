About Us

The Bourounis Family —
Fort Wayne's Own

ALL Lawn Care Service started with a simple idea: every property deserves to look its best, and every customer deserves to be treated like a neighbor — because in Fort Wayne, you probably are.

George Bourounis and his crew have been showing up across Fort Wayne for over 15 years. We handle everything from weekly mowing routes to complete landscape installations, and we take the same pride in a shopping center parking lot that we do in a front yard on Brandywine Drive.

With 200+ Google reviews, an A+ BBB rating, and Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite recognition in two Fort Wayne neighborhoods — our reputation speaks for itself.
